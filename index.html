Frequently Asked Questions

Read quick answers to routine procedural and financial questions regarding hiring Vanguard Partners legal counsel.

We offer free, zero-obligation initial evaluations for Personal Injury and Estate Planning cases. For highly complex corporate consulting or ongoing contract auditing, standard advisory consultation hourly fees may apply. Contact our reception staff for precise estimates.
Please bring all documents pertaining to your legal inquiry. For personal injury claims, bring police reports, medical files, insurance coverages, and incident photographs. For corporate disputes, bring existing partnership agreements, disputed contracts, and related text correspondence.
Under contingency arrangements (primarily Personal Injury litigations), you pay absolutely no upfront retainer fees. Our compensation is a set percentage of the total court verdict or out-of-court settlement we win on your behalf. If we do not successfully recover funds for you, we charge zero attorney fees.
Resolution duration depends heavily on the complexity of the trial. Straightforward real estate contracts can finalize in weeks, whereas high-conflict divorces or complex corporate patent infringement lawsuits in federal courts can stretch from 12 to 24 months. We aim to settle cases efficiently without compromising on total compensation.
